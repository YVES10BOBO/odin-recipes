Loaded Steak Fries

 
piemonade is concocted of a slice of pie stuck in a glass of lemonande with
lots of ice cubes in it and lemon wedge on the side

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar(can reduce to 3/4 cup)
  • 1 cup water (for the simple syrup)
  • 1 cup fresh squeeze lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 cups cold water , to dilute
  • 1 pound sirloin filet steak
  1.                 start with porportionals of 1 cup of lemon juice .
                 reduce the sugar amountif you are using meyer lemons or if you like lemonade less sweet .
                 ( i usually use 3/4 cup of sugar.)

            
  2. Meanwhile, make the simple syrup , combine with the lemons , juice and the add more water (and ice )
                dilute the lemonade to your taste
